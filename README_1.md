# Business Sales Performance Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, and regional performance. The goal is to generate insights that support better business decision-making.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Google Sheets / Microsoft Excel
- Canva (for dashboard and report)

## 📈 Analysis Performed
- Sales trend analysis over time
- Category-wise performance analysis
- Regional sales comparison
- Profitability analysis

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Sales show an overall increasing trend with some fluctuations
- Technology category generates the highest revenue
- West region performs the best in terms of sales
- Some products have high sales but low profit margins

## 💡 Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing products in the Technology category
- Expand business in high-performing regions like the West
- Improve cost and pricing strategies to increase profit margins
- Strengthen performance in low-performing categories

## 📄 Conclusion
The analysis provides valuable insights into business performance and helps in making data-driven decisions to improve growth and profitability.
